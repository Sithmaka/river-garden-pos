✅ Why They Look Different (This is Correct!)
The screen preview is intentionally LARGER (400px wide, 11-24pt fonts) for comfortable reading on monitors. The printed receipt is SMALLER (63.5mm / 240px wide, 8-12pt fonts) to fit the Xprinter XP-80C thermal paper.

When you print: Your browser automatically scales the receipt to fit the 2.5" paper width. The content stays the same - just the display size changes. This is normal and correct!
